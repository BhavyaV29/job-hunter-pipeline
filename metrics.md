## Conversion checks

Calculate from cumulative totals:

```text
reply rate             = replies / applications
recruiter conversion   = recruiter screens / replies
technical conversion   = tech screens / recruiter screens
onsite conversion      = onsites / tech screens
offer conversion       = offers / onsites
referral reply rate    = replies attributable to referrals / referral asks
```

After **30 quality applications**:

- fewer than 2 replies → fix targeting and resume-variant distribution; do not add sources;
- replies but weak recruiter-to-tech conversion → tighten positioning and claim defense;
- tech screens but weak advancement → move time to AI-free DSA, system design, and mocks.

After two full weeks, the minimum operating proof is **60 quality applications, 30 referral
asks, logged follow-ups, active oral drills, and one external mock in week 2**.
